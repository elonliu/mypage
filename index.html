About Me

I'm currently a Ph.D. student at the Institute for Advanced Study, Tsinghua University. I'm doing research in Computer Graphics, majorly focused on facial animation. It's currently the fourth year of my Ph.D. career. My adviser is Prof. Baining Guo, and I'm mentored by Xin Tong in the Joint Ph.D. Program with Microsoft Research Asia.

I received my B.S. Degree in Automation and Second B.S. Degree in Economics from Tsinghua University in 2012.
